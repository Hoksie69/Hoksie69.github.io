The game

Brawlhalla is an online platform game. There are many different characters with their own special abilities. The base of the game is to fight against other people real-time pvp.

When you get in a game your goal is to get a weapon and to beat up your opponent. The more damage you take, the easier it is for your opponent to kill you, but fortunatly this works also the other way. After a battle, you gain experience and coins. With coins you can buy new Heroes and with experience you can unlock adjustements for your heroes.

This is ofcourse a very short summary of the game and you'll see that there is a lot of cool stuff you can do while in-game.
